LetterBox Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 27, 2026

LetterBox is a local-first application designed for professionals to manage their newsletters as a tactical intelligence stream. We operate on a Local-Only architecture, meaning your sensitive transmissions never leave your device.

Google API Limited Use Disclosure: LetterBox's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Service User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

1. Access to Google User Data

LetterBox requests the gmail.readonly scope to provide its core service. This access is strictly limited to:

  • Reading the subject, sender, and body of your emails to identify newsletters.
  • Providing automated summaries and engagement analytics locally.
  • Enabling the Workbench feature to capture notes from your feed.

2. Data Handling & Security

LetterBox implements a high-security, local-first data model:

  • No Central Server: We do not operate an application server that stores or mirrors your Gmail data. Application backend logic is non-existent by design.
  • Local Storage: All transmissions, engagement metrics, and tactical notes are stored exclusively in your browser's persistent storage (IndexedDB).
  • Zero Sharing: Your data is never sold, shared, or transferred to third-party services. All data stays under your direct control on your own machine.

3. Use of Information

Information accessed via Google APIs is used solely to enhance your newsletter reading experience within the LetterBox dashboard. We do not use this data for advertising, profiling, or any secondary purposes beyond the dashboard's explicit features.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

Because LetterBox stores data locally, you have absolute control over your information:

  • Deletion: You can purge all data at any time by clearing your browser's site data or using the "Logout" feature in the navigation bar.
  • Revocation: You can revoke LetterBox's access to your Google Account at any time via the Google Security settings page.
