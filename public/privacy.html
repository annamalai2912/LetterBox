LetterBox Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 27, 2026

LetterBox is an "Intelligence Command Center" designed for managing newsletters. We prioritize your privacy through a local-first architecture.

1. Data Collection & Use

LetterBox accesses your Gmail data via the gmail.readonly scope. This access is used restrictedly to:

  • Retrieve and display newsletter content within the app dashboard.
  • Analyze newsletter frequency and engagement scores.
  • Enable extraction of tactical insights into your private Workbench.
Google API Limited Use Disclosure:
LetterBox's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Service User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

2. Local Storage

Crucially: All data retrieved from Gmail (messages, senders, and content) along with your personal notes and sketches are stored exclusively in your browser's IndexedDB. We do not operate a central server that stores your personal data.

3. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your data to outside parties. Your data never leaves your local device unless you manually copy it to your clipboard.
